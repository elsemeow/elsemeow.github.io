A week without normal sleep was not in vain. I released a reimagined version of my canvas-based game engine with relative scaling — [MeowGine](https://github.com/elsemeow/meowgine){:target="_blank"}. As well I gave up trying to write my own collision detection for complex objects and just took Jim Riecken's [SAT.js](https://github.com/jriecken/sat-js){:target="_blank"} as a base for `Entity` class. "Pens" available under the [meowgine-v2 tag](https://codepen.io/tag/meowgine-v2){:target="_blank"} on CodePen.

The first version of MeowGine was released on October 21, 2021. [MeowGine v1.2.0-alpha](https://github.com/elsemeow/meowgine/tree/v1.2.0-alpha){:target="_blank"} is `deprecated` but still available for use. Old "Pens" on which I tested the project till `v1.2.0-alpha` are available under the [meowgine tag](https://codepen.io/tag/meowgine){:target="_blank"} on CodePen.
