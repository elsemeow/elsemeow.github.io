Assigned to create merit certificates for colleagues, I dove into the design task. After receiving a link to a document with the achievements list, I opted for a four-tiered system: 0-2 - **Common**, 3-4 - **Rare**, 5-6 - **Epic**, and 7 (all achievements) - **Legendary**. Spent about 6 hours on this, focusing on crafting visually appealing certificates. See the result on the screenshot below.
